Welcome

The workout-log app isn't just another fitness app. It's is a practical preview of an app that's helps you reaching your goals while combining a social aspect.

Compare to others

Compare yourself with your friends to learn from each other and to compete with them. If one of you lost track of his or her training you can support and cheer each other on

Steady progress

Progression in strength training is probably the most important factor when it comes to building muscle and strength. Constant muscle building can only take place when the training load increases progressively. Workout-log helps you track this progress easily.

Do not overcomplicate!

Put the focus on the important thing, which is perfectly possible through our simple design. Also, the app is free of any bullshit. No subscriptions, no ads, perfect for focused training.
